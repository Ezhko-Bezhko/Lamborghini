The first Lamborghini car

The first Lamborghini car, the 350 GT, was launched in 1964, just a year after the company was founded. It set the tone for Lamborghini's reputation for luxury, style, and performance.

Miura - The First Supercar:

The Lamborghini Miura, introduced in 1966, is often considered the world's first supercar due to its mid-engine layout, which became the standard for high-performance sports cars.
